Method A: Netlify Drag-and-Drop (Easiest — Recommended)
1
Create a Free Netlify Account
  • Go to netlify.com and click "Sign up free"
  • Sign up with your email (recommended) or GitHub account
  • Verify your email and log in
💡 Netlify's free "Starter" plan is completely free with no expiry. You don't need a credit card.
2
Prepare Your Website Files
  • Make sure you have all files: index.html, shared.css, services.html, about.html, australia.html, india.html, uae.html, team.html, blog.html, contact.html, analytics.html
  • Before uploading, do your contact replacements (Step 3 below)
  • Put all files into one folder on your computer (e.g. fintex-website)
⚠️ All files must be in the same folder. Do not put them in sub-folders or links will break.
3
Update Your Contact Details (Important)
Before uploading, replace the placeholder values in your HTML files. Open each .html file in a text editor (Notepad on Windows, TextEdit on Mac, or VS Code). Use Find & Replace (Ctrl+H / Cmd+H) across all files:
  • REPLACE_AU_EMAIL → your Australia email (e.g. arun@tensquare.com.au)
  • REPLACE_AU_WHATSAPP → AU WhatsApp number, digits only, with country code (e.g. 61412345678)
  • REPLACE_INDIA_EMAIL → India email address
  • REPLACE_INDIA_WHATSAPP → India WhatsApp (e.g. 919876543210)
  • REPLACE_UAE_WHATSAPP → UAE WhatsApp (e.g. 971557282794) — this is already set for the UAE number
💡 If you don't have the India/AU numbers yet, leave the placeholders. The buttons will still display but won't link anywhere until you update them later and re-upload.
⚠️ VS Code (free download) makes it very easy: open the folder, press Ctrl+Shift+H and replace across all files at once.
4
Deploy on Netlify — Drag & Drop
  • Log in to app.netlify.com
  • On the main dashboard you'll see a grey dotted box: "Drag and drop your site folder here"
  • Open your file explorer, find your fintex-website folder, and drag the entire folder onto that box
  • Netlify will upload all files and give you a URL like https://amazing-name-123.netlify.app
  • Your website is now live! Click the URL to check it
💡 The whole process takes about 30 seconds once you drag the folder.
5
Connect Your Custom Domain (fintexglobal.com)
  • In Netlify, go to your site → Domain settings → Add custom domain
  • Enter fintexglobal.com and click Verify
  • Netlify will show you DNS records to add (usually 2 records: an A record and a CNAME)
  • Log in to wherever you bought your domain (GoDaddy, Namecheap, etc.) → DNS Management
  • Add the records exactly as Netlify shows
  • DNS changes take 10 minutes to 24 hours to fully propagate
  • Netlify provides free HTTPS (SSL certificate) automatically once the domain is connected
💡 If you bought your domain on GoDaddy, search "GoDaddy add DNS record" for a visual guide — it's just 3 clicks.
6
Update Your Website Later (After First Deploy)
Whenever you need to update any file (e.g. add new contact details, update a service price):
  • Edit the HTML file on your computer
  • Go to Netlify → your site → Deploys tab
  • Drag the entire updated folder onto the deploy box again
  • Netlify will detect which files changed and re-deploy (takes ~10 seconds)
Method B: Cloudflare Pages (Fastest CDN, also Free)
B
Deploy on Cloudflare Pages
  • Go to pages.cloudflare.com and create a free account
  • Click Create a project → Direct Upload
  • Give your project a name (e.g. fintex-global)
  • Upload all your website files
  • Click Deploy site — you'll get a .pages.dev URL
  • For custom domain: go to your project → Custom domains → add fintexglobal.com
💡 Cloudflare has data centers in Dubai, Sydney and Mumbai — ideal for the AU + India + UAE audience.
Adding Real Analytics (Optional Upgrades)
G
Add Google Analytics 4 (Free)
  • Go to analytics.google.com → Create account → Create property
  • Select "Web", enter your website URL
  • Google gives you a code snippet like: <script async src="...gtag/js?id=G-XXXXXXXX">
  • Open each of your .html files and paste this snippet just before the </head> tag
  • Re-upload your files to Netlify
  • Traffic data will appear in Google Analytics within 24 hours
💡 Google Analytics tracks visitors from all regions automatically — no extra setup for AU/India/UAE.
F
Enable Form Notifications via Netlify Forms (Free)
To receive contact form submissions directly in your email inbox:
  • In contact.html, change the form submission to use Netlify Forms by adding netlify attribute to your form tag
  • In Netlify dashboard → your site → Forms → enable email notifications
  • Enter your email and every form submission will arrive in your inbox instantly
  • Netlify Forms free tier: 100 submissions/month (upgrade for more)
💡 Alternatively, use Formspree.io (free, 50 submissions/month) — just change the form action URL.
Updating Contact Details Later
📞
How to Add/Update Regional Contact Numbers Later
When you get your Australia and India WhatsApp numbers:
  • Download VS Code for free from code.visualstudio.com
  • Open VS Code → File → Open Folder → select your fintex-website folder
  • Press Ctrl+Shift+H (Find & Replace in all files)
  • In the "Search" box type: REPLACE_AU_WHATSAPP
  • In the "Replace" box type your actual number: e.g. 61412345678
  • Click "Replace All" — it updates every file at once
  • Repeat for REPLACE_INDIA_WHATSAPP, REPLACE_AU_EMAIL, REPLACE_INDIA_EMAIL
  • Save all files and re-upload the folder to Netlify
💡 WhatsApp numbers must be in international format without + or spaces. Australia: 61 + number. India: 91 + number. UAE: 971 + number.
